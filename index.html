Sunrise

Prototype angle

Pre-test onboarding for uncertain users

This concept widens Sunrise's entry funnel by helping people who suspect a sleep problem but are not yet ready to buy a test kit. It uses emotionally resonant onboarding, lightweight guidance, and a clear final recommendation without drifting into diagnosis.

User problem

"I feel exhausted and something may be wrong, but I do not know if this is serious enough to act on."

Product move

Start with empathy and risk framing, then use a short guided check to create enough confidence to continue.

Best-case outcome

The user reaches an "aha" moment and feels safe buying the Sunrise test kit instead of abandoning the funnel.
